Best Places to Travel

Six stunning destinations, each with its own vibrant personality

Japan
⛩

Kyoto

Ancient temples draped in vermillion, bamboo groves swaying in whispered breezes, and centuries of tradition alive in every tea ceremony.

Best: Mar-May Temples & Gardens
Greece
☀

Santorini

Whitewashed villages tumbling down volcanic cliffs into the deepest blue sea. Sunsets here redefine the meaning of golden hour.

Best: Jun-Sep Islands & Sunsets
Argentina
⛰

Patagonia

Where jagged granite peaks pierce endless skies and glaciers carve through valleys of emerald. Nature at its most raw and untamed.

Best: Nov-Mar Hiking & Glaciers
Morocco
✦

Marrakech

A kaleidoscope of spice-scented souks, intricate zellige tilework, and riads hiding secret gardens behind ancient ochre walls.

Best: Mar-May Markets & Culture
Iceland
❄

Reykjavik

Gateway to a land of fire and ice where geysers erupt, northern lights dance, and geothermal pools steam under vast Arctic skies.

Best: Sep-Mar Aurora & Hot Springs
Italy
🎋

Amalfi Coast

Pastel villages cling to dramatic cliffs above turquoise waters. Lemon groves perfume the air as winding roads reveal breathtaking vistas.

Best: May-Sep Coastal & Cuisine
Czech Republic
🏰

Prague

A fairy-tale city of Gothic spires and Baroque palaces, where cobblestone lanes wind past astronomical clocks and the Vltava River reflects centuries of art and revolution.

Best: Jun-Sep Architecture & History
Peru
⛰

Machu Picchu

The lost citadel of the Inca, perched among the clouds on a razor-thin ridge, where ancient stone terraces cascade into mist and the silence holds the memory of an empire.

Best: May-Sep Ruins & Trekking
