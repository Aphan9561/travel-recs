🏯 KYOTO, JAPAN 🏯
HOT!

~*~ Kyoto ~*~

Ancient temples, beautiful gardens, and amazing food!! Kyoto is the cultural heart of Japan. Visit during cherry blossom season for an INCREDIBLE experience!!!

Best Time: March - May (Cherry Blossoms!!)

Must See: Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kinkaku-ji

Rating: ★★★★★

*.*.*.*.*.*.*.*.*.*.*
🌴 SANTORINI, GREECE 🌴
NEW!

~*~ Santorini ~*~

White-washed buildings, blue domes, and the most GORGEOUS sunsets you've ever seen!! This Greek island is a MUST for any traveler!!

Best Time: June - September

Must See: Oia Sunset, Red Beach, Akrotiri

Rating: ★★★★★

~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
⛰ PATAGONIA ⛰
COOL!

~*~ Patagonia ~*~

For the ADVENTUROUS traveler!! Massive glaciers, towering mountains, and endless wilderness. Torres del Paine will blow your mind!!!

Best Time: November - March

Must See: Torres del Paine, Perito Moreno Glacier

Rating: ★★★★☆

=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
🌞 MARRAKECH, MOROCCO 🌞
WOW!

~*~ Marrakech ~*~

Get lost in the magical souks and medinas!! The colors, the spices, the architecture... Marrakech is a FEAST for all your senses!!!

Best Time: March - May, Sept - Nov

Must See: Jemaa el-Fnaa, Majorelle Garden

Rating: ★★★★★

*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*
❄ REYKJAVIK, ICELAND ❄
EPIC!

~*~ Reykjavik ~*~

Land of fire and ice!! See the Northern Lights, soak in hot springs, and explore volcanoes. Iceland is OUT OF THIS WORLD!!!

Best Time: June - Aug (Midnight Sun) or Winter (Northern Lights)

Must See: Blue Lagoon, Golden Circle, Northern Lights

Rating: ★★★★★

*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*
🌊 AMALFI COAST, ITALY 🌊
FAV!

~*~ Amalfi Coast ~*~

Dramatic cliffs, pastel villages, and the BEST pasta and limoncello ever!! Drive the winding coastal road for views that will make you CRY!!

Best Time: May - September

Must See: Positano, Ravello, Path of the Gods

Rating: ★★★★★
